Our Story

Artisanal Mastery — Redefining Unisex Grooming Since Day One

✦ The Legacy ✦

Where Luxury Meets Artistry

Founded in 2008 by master stylist Ashraf Salmani, Ashraf's Unisex Salon & Academy was born from a singular vision — to create a space where impeccable grooming meets artistic expression, without boundaries of gender.

What started as a small boutique salon with just 3 chairs has grown into Mumbai's most trusted destination for premium unisex grooming.

Today, we're proud to have trained over 1,500+ aspiring stylists and served 50,000+ delighted clients.

✦ Our Purpose ✦

Why We Exist
